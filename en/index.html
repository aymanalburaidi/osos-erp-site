Security & isolation

Your company’s data… for your company only

Isolation is not a setting we toggled · it is the architecture the system was built on from the first line.

  • Database-level isolation

    Every record is tagged to your company and protected by row-level security (RLS) · no other tenant can ever reach it.

  • Fine-grained permissions

    Roles, groups and segregation of duties: the cashier can’t approve their own request, and everyone sees only their own scope.

  • A complete audit trail

    Every action is recorded: who did it, when, and what changed · ready for review at any time.

  • Trusted cloud infrastructure

    Encryption in transit and at rest, with continuous database backups.

FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Can’t find your question? Message us on WhatsApp and we’ll answer directly.

Is the system compliant with ZATCA requirements?

Yes. Phase-2 e-invoicing (integration, reporting and clearance via the FATOORA platform) is built in, with a QR code on every invoice and 15% VAT computed and posted automatically.

Can I enable only specific modules?

Yes · the system is fully modular. Start with what you need (say accounting and HR) and add the rest later; you pay only for what you enable.

How long does setup take?

A trial environment within one working day, and go-live within days depending on your data readiness · our import center helps you migrate your existing data.

Does it support English?

The platform is Arabic-first (RTL from the root, not an afterthought translation) and supports English for bilingual teams.

What about our data if we decide to leave?

Your data is always yours · you get a complete export on request, in standard, usable formats.

Does it work on mobile?

It runs in any modern browser with nothing to install, and employee screens (attendance and requests) are designed for mobile first.
